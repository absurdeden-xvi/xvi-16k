# Early Product Questionnaire: Selected Answers

This document preserves a small, curated selection from the creator's original Chinese product questionnaire. The questions were prepared to support product thinking; every answer quoted below was typed by the creator before the current implementation took shape. English translations are provided for judging, while the Chinese originals remain the source of truth.

The complete raw questionnaire is kept in the creator's private archive because it also contains unfinished thoughts, personal notes, and ideas outside the current product scope.

## Product purpose

**Question: In one sentence, what should XVI help users accomplish?**

> Help writers turn text into long images. I hope to bring better visual judgment to the market instead of offering something repetitive, or something that only copies the classics without knowing how to adapt. It can also serve people who want to keep a diary or record their lives.

**中文原答：**

> 帮助文手进行长图转换。希望有更好的审美出现在市场中，而不是千篇一律、或者一味复刻经典，不懂得变通的东西。当然，也适合有需要写日记，记录生活等的人。

## The most important feeling

**Question: What is the most important feeling when someone uses XVI?**

> Freedom. Freedom will always be the most important part. How free is a kite? Only the wind knows. I want to begin with a website that feels complete, while keeping the basic functions free.

**中文原答：**

> 自由。自由永远是最重要的一环。风筝多自由，只有风知道。我希望是一个完美的网页起步，另外，基础功能绝对免费。

## Why another tool

**Question: Why would a user not simply use Canva, Figma, screenshots, or another long-image tool?**

> For domestic users, especially people who are not professional designers, Figma is not a convenient choice, and Canva is similar. The goal is automatic but flexible typesetting: generating the long image instead of asking the user to begin by learning graphic design from zero. The important part is producing what a creator needs quickly for publishing across platforms. We need stronger visual judgment and a more concise process.

**中文原答：**

> 对于国内用户（尤其非专业设计者）来说，Figma 并不是方便的选择，Canva 也差不多。我们的目标功能是进行“全自动”自由排版，自动生成长图，而不是从零开始接触美工设计。重点在于快速地生成创作者需要的长图，方便在各平台发布。我们需要更高级的审美，更精简的流程。

## Ideal workflow

**Question: What is the ideal primary workflow?**

> Enter the text, generate automatically, optionally choose a template, adjust with an immediate preview, and export.

**中文原答：**

> 输入文本，自动生成，（可选模板），即时调整与预览，导出。

## Automation and control

**Question: Do users need automation or precise control?**

> Both should be available so the product can adapt to different people.

**中文原答：**

> 都可以应用，适应不同人群的需求。

## Visual direction

**Question: Describe the ideal XVI in three to five words.**

> Free, minimal, low-saturation, refined, and lively.

**中文原答：**

> 自由、简约、低饱和、高级、灵动。

**Question: What must it never feel like?**

> Cluttered, cumbersome, redundant, or tacky.

**中文原答：**

> 冗杂、繁琐、多余、土。

## Publishing needs

**Question: Should XVI export one long image or automatically split it into multiple images?**

> It should depend on the user's needs. Both should be possible after the user defines their preference.

**中文原答：**

> 看用户的要求，自定义偏好后都可以做到。
